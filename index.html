Q & A

Who runs Qureshi Media?

I’m Mohammed Adam Qureshi, 54, based in NYC. I’ve been building and shipping my own products for about a decade. No committee. No layers. Just me and the stack.

What does Qureshi Media actually do?

Qureshi Media is a product development / AI engineering shop. I:

  • Design, build, and launch new products and MVPs.
  • Use AI heavily to move fast from idea → prototype → live.
  • Think through go‑to‑market, not just “build the app”.

My bias is toward shipping and learning from the market, not endless planning.

Can I hire you?

Maybe. I am not looking for a job. I am not looking for “more work.” I am interested in owners with real skin in the game.

If I don’t like the industry or the idea, I won’t take it. No hard feelings.

What does it cost to work together?

Idea breakdown call: $1,000 for 45 minutes.

I’ll tear down your idea, pressure‑test it, and outline what I’d actually build and how I’d take it to market.

Build: Minimum $30k to ship a proper MVP. If that number scares you, you’re not ready for me.

What kind of founders do you work with?

Owners and early‑stage startups who:

  • Care about speed and shipping over decks and drama.
  • Make decisions directly (no 10‑person steering committees).
  • Understand that product + distribution are the same conversation.

If you’re an operator who wants to get something into the market fast, we’ll probably get along.

How do you work?

I’m a one‑man shop using AI as leverage. That means:

  • Short feedback loops. You talk to me, not a project manager.
  • Hands‑on with product, UX, engineering, and go‑to‑market.
  • Focus on shipping something small, sharp, and real — not a 12‑month “platform”.
What happens if I pitch you something?

I’ll look at:

  • Do I like this space?
  • Is there a clear, sharp MVP?
  • Is there a believable path to customers?

I may say no. I’ve seen a lot. I’m okay passing. You should be too.
